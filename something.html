Enter the Style Sheet

I always name my first stylesheet main.css and of course I put it in the css folder.

Next, I link to the stylesheet in my HTML page. This is necessary in order to load the styles for your site.

<head>
  <title>Visit KC</title>
  <link href="css/main.css" rel="stylesheet" />
</head>
